The movie became the highest grossing movie of that year and the second movie of all time to cross the $1 billion mark. Futhermore the movie recieved a lot of accolades and awards. Changes made from the book

The movie adaptation had certain changes in regards to the book. The greatest of those changes was the removal of the subplot Scouring of the Shire where the hobbits return to the Shire after the defeat of Sauron to find it taken over by Saruman. Some other changes are the shuffling of events from the second book to this movie and the life of the characters after the ending. See here for more information
